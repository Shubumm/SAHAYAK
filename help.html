Help & Support

Welcome to the Help Center! We’re here to assist you. Whether you’re a student navigating through our content, an educator uploading resources, or an institute interested in partnering with us, this page is designed to guide you through common questions and issues.

1. Getting Started

How do I access content on Sahayak?

To access the content, log in with your institute-provided credentials. Once logged in, you can explore various sections such as Topper Videos, Quiz Bot, Alumni Talks, and more.

How can I search for specific videos or resources?

Use the search bar located at the top of the page to find specific videos, documents, or other learning materials. You can search by topic, subject, or contributor.

How do I leave comments or feedback on videos?

Scroll down to the video’s feedback section and type your comment in the provided text box. Click "Submit" to share your thoughts or ask questions.

2. Account Management

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we’ll send you instructions on how to reset your password.

Can I change my registered email address?

Currently, email addresses are tied to your institute’s database. If you need to update your email, please contact your institute’s administration.

How can I update my profile information?

Go to your profile by clicking on your name in the top-right corner, then select "Edit Profile". Make the necessary changes and save.

3. Video and Content Issues

Why can’t I play a video?

Ensure your internet connection is stable. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or using a different browser. If you still face problems, please contact support.

The video is not loading properly, what should I do?

Try clearing your browser’s cache or use incognito mode. If this doesn't help, report the issue through the contact form below.

Can I download videos or notes?

Currently, videos are stream-only to protect intellectual property. However, notes linked to videos can be downloaded for offline use.

4. For Educators and Institutes

How can I upload content?

If you’re an authorized educator, log in to your educator dashboard and select "Upload Content". Follow the prompts to upload videos, notes, or other resources.

What are the guidelines for content submission?

All content should be educational, relevant to the curriculum, and adhere to our quality standards. For detailed guidelines, visit our Content Submission Guidelines.

How can my institute partner with Sahayak?

Interested in a partnership? Visit our Partnerships Page to learn more about the benefits and fill out the partnership inquiry form.

5. Technical Support

Having trouble accessing your account?

Double-check your login details and try again. If you still can’t access your account, please contact our support team with your registered email address.

Experiencing bugs or glitches?

We’re constantly working to improve our platform. If you encounter any issues, please report them through our Bug Report Form or contact support.

Browser compatibility issues?

Sahayak works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Ensure your browser is up-to-date for the best experience.

6. Contact Us

If you still need help, our support team is here to assist you with any issues or questions you may have. Email us at support@sahayak.com. We’ll get back to you within 24-48 hours.
