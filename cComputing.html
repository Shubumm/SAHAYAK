What is Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is like having a big computer that you can use from anywhere in the world as long as you have an internet connection. You can save your pictures, videos, and documents on this big computer and access them from your phone, tablet, or computer. It's like having a magic box that holds all your important stuff!
